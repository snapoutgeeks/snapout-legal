What SNAPOUT does

SNAPOUT monitors the apps you choose to limit — Instagram, YouTube, or any others you add — and intervenes when you've spent too much time scrolling. Instead of just tracking usage, it interrupts the habit loop with science-backed warnings and real alternatives you chose for yourself.

Features

📜
Personal Commitment Sign a covenant with yourself. Set your daily target, your pledges, and what you'll do instead of scrolling.
⚡
Session Warnings Full-screen intervention when your session ends. Brain science, quotes, and a 60-second decision window.
🔀
The Switch During cooldown, SNAPOUT shows the activities you chose — reading, walking, calling someone. Your own promise, reflected back.
🧘
Monk Mode Lock your phone completely for focused time. Choose which apps stay accessible for emergencies.
🌙
Night Shield Automatically blocks monitored apps during your sleep hours. Protects your melatonin and your mornings.
👨‍👧
Guardian Mode Parents can set up a protected profile for children with daily caps, difficulty levels, and PIN protection.
🏅
Badges & Streaks 20 badges across four rarity tiers. Track streaks, hours saved, cooldowns completed, and more.
☁️
Optional Cloud Backup Sign in with Google to back up your commitment, streak, and settings. Restores automatically on new devices.
