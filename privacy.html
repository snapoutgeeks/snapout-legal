Privacy policy

Last updated: May 31, 2026

Short version: SNAPOUT does not sell your data, show you ads, or track you for any commercial purpose. Usage tracking happens entirely on your device. The only data that ever leaves your device is your settings backup — and only if you choose to sign in with Google. Sign-in is completely optional.

1. Who we are

SNAPOUT is an Android application developed and maintained by The SNAPOUT team ("we", "us", "our"). You can reach us at snapoutapporiginal@gmail.com.

2. What information SNAPOUT accesses

App usage statistics

SNAPOUT uses Android's PACKAGE_USAGE_STATS permission to detect which app is in the foreground. This lets the app know when you open Instagram, YouTube, or any app you've chosen to monitor.

This is read in real time and never transmitted anywhere. We track only cumulative time on apps you explicitly selected — and that tracking stays on your device.

Display over other apps

SNAPOUT uses the SYSTEM_ALERT_WINDOW permission to show full-screen warning overlays when your session limit is reached. This is not used to read what you do in other apps.

Vibration

Used to create haptic feedback during cooldown warnings. No data is involved.

Foreground service

SNAPOUT runs a foreground service to continuously monitor app usage. A persistent notification is shown while it runs, as required by Android. The service only performs the monitoring and intervention functions described above.

Boot completed

SNAPOUT uses RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED to restart its monitoring service after your device reboots, so you don't have to relaunch the app manually.

Google Sign-In (optional)

SNAPOUT offers optional Google Sign-In powered by Firebase Authentication (Google LLC). Sign-in is never required. If you choose to sign in, your Google account email and a unique Firebase user ID are used to identify your backup. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or any other Google data.

Cloud backup (optional, signed-in users only)

If you sign in, your settings, commitment data, streak, monitored app list, selected Switch activities, and badge progress are backed up to Google Firestore (hosted in the Mumbai, India region). This data is stored under your unique Firebase user ID and is only accessible by you. You can delete it at any time from the Profile screen. We use Firebase solely for this backup — not for analytics, advertising, or any other purpose.

3. What we store on your device

SNAPOUT stores the following locally using Android's SharedPreferences:

  • Session limits, cooldown duration, and other settings
  • List of apps you selected to monitor
  • Daily usage totals for monitored apps
  • Your commitment form answers (name, target hours, pledges)
  • Your Switch activity selections
  • Streak counts, badges, cooldowns completed, hours saved
  • Night Shield settings and Guardian mode settings

If you are not signed in, this data never leaves your device. It is stored in your app's private data directory, accessible only by SNAPOUT.

If you are signed in, the above data is also backed up to Firestore as described in Section 2.

4. What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your data to anyone, ever.
  • We do not show ads inside SNAPOUT.
  • We do not use Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics, Mixpanel, or any analytics service.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs or track you for ad targeting.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not access your contacts, photos, files, or messages.
  • We do not read the content of notifications in other apps.
  • We do not use crash reporting services that send data off-device.
  • We do not share data with any third party except Firebase (Google LLC) for the optional cloud backup described above.

5. Firebase and Google

We use two Firebase services — Firebase Authentication and Cloud Firestore — solely to power the optional sign-in and cloud backup feature. Firebase is operated by Google LLC. When you sign in, your data is subject to Google's Privacy Policy in addition to ours. If you do not sign in, no data is sent to Firebase or Google through SNAPOUT.

6. Data sharing

We do not share your personal data with any third parties for commercial purposes. The only external service that receives any data is Firebase (Google LLC), and only if you choose to sign in. We have no advertising partners, data brokers, or analytics providers.

7. Data retention and deletion

Local data: Uninstalling SNAPOUT or clearing app storage deletes all local data immediately.

Cloud data (signed-in users): You can delete your cloud data at any time from Profile → Delete Account. This permanently removes your Firestore document and Firebase account. We do not retain any data after deletion.

8. Children's privacy

SNAPOUT includes a Guardian Mode designed for parents to set up child profiles. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The child profile stores only a nickname, difficulty level, and daily cap — locally on the parent's device and, if the parent is signed in, in their own Firestore document under their account. No child account is created in Firebase.

9. Your rights

Under laws such as GDPR (Europe), CCPA (California), and the DPDP Act (India), you have the following rights:

  • Right to access: View all local data in the app. View cloud data by contacting us.
  • Right to delete: Clear app storage to delete local data. Use Profile → Delete Account to delete cloud data.
  • Right to portability: Contact us at the email below and we will provide your data.
  • Right to object: Disable Usage Access permission or uninstall to stop all processing.

Contact us at snapoutapporiginal@gmail.com for any rights requests.

10. Security

Local data is stored in Android's sandboxed app-private storage, protected from other apps. Cloud data in Firestore is protected by Firebase Security Rules that ensure only you can access your own data — we enforce this at the database level. We strongly recommend keeping your device secured with a lock screen and keeping Android updated.

11. Changes to this policy

If we change how SNAPOUT handles data we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify users through the app before the change takes effect. The current version of this policy always lives at this URL.

12. Contact

Questions about this policy or how SNAPOUT handles your data? Email us at snapoutapporiginal@gmail.com. We read every message.
